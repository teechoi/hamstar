## Content Series

### 1. RACE DAY — Pre-race hype (post 2–3 hrs before each race)

One clip per hamster, 15–20 seconds, posted separately with character-specific copy.

**Dash:**
> *Dash the red hamster warming up on a treadmill, sunflower seeds flying, crowd cheering in background, neon racing lights, cinematic slow-mo, dramatic music build, text overlay "RACE DAY"*

**Flash:**
> *Flash the pink hamster bouncing off walls of a neon race paddock, sparks flying, camera shaking, erratic chaotic movement, glitch effects, text overlay "CHAOS INCOMING"*

**Turbo:**
> *Turbo the cyan hamster staring directly into camera in a dark locker room, single spotlight, slow zoom in, no expression, dramatic orchestral sting, text overlay "THE DARK HORSE"*

---

### 2. THE ODDS — Betting card drop (post 1 hr before each race)

High repost potential. Update percentages to match real cheer pool data if available.

> *Cinematic sports broadcast card, three hamster portraits side by side, glowing probability bars beneath each, dramatic music, slow camera push into the favorite, overlay text: DASH 45% · FLASH 33% · TURBO 22%*

**Caption formula:** `Race #[N] odds are live. $HAMSTAR pool opens now. Who are you backing? 🐹`

---

### 3. CHARACTER DIARIES — Personality content (2–3× per week)

Works on non-race days. Builds parasocial attachment and character identity.

**Dash series:**
- Dash eating sunflower seeds at a tiny kitchen table, reading fan mail
- Dash signing autographs for other hamsters after a win
- Dash training montage — determination, hustle, Eye of the Tiger energy
- Dash nervously pacing before a big race

**Flash series:**
- Flash accidentally breaking things in the paddock (apologetically)
- Flash's "strategy session" on a whiteboard that makes no sense
- Flash napping in a bizarre location (on top of the trophy, inside the wheel)
- Flash watching his own race footage and not understanding what he did

**Turbo series:**
- Turbo alone in a dark gym at 3am, methodically training
- Turbo watching race footage on a tiny laptop, taking notes with a tiny pen
- Turbo ignoring everyone at the pre-race press conference
- Turbo in an ice bath post-race, completely expressionless

---

### 4. RACE RECAP — Post-race results (post immediately after)

Highest engagement window. People just watched and have money on the line.

**Winner clip:**
> *[Winning hamster name] crossing a finish line in slow motion, confetti explosion, crowd erupting, golden trophy lowering from above, cinematic golden backlighting, text overlay "RACE #[N] — CHAMPION"*

**Upset/dark horse win:**
> *Turbo crossing finish line while Dash and Flash stare in disbelief, dramatic reversal sting, crowd chaos, replays of the upset moment, text overlay "NOBODY SAW THAT COMING"*

**Loser moment** (memeable, builds character):
> *Dash slumped against the track wall, sunflower seed falling from his paw, slow zoom out, sad violin, text overlay "NEXT TIME, CHAMP 🫡"*

**Caption formula for winner:** `Race #[N] is over. [Winner] takes the wheel. $[AMOUNT] distributed to [N] winners. Next race: [DATE]. 🏆`

---

### 5. RIVALRY CUTS — Head-to-head drama (weekly)

Sports media runs on rivalries. Build these hard.

**Dash vs Flash (Order vs Chaos):**
> *Split screen: Dash carefully studying a detailed race map on the left, Flash eating the race map on the right, both staring at camera, dramatic rivalry music, text: "DIFFERENT APPROACHES. ONE WINNER."*

**Turbo vs the field (The Loner):**
> *Turbo walking through the paddock alone while Dash and Flash laugh together in background, ignoring everyone, cold thousand-yard stare, dramatic slow motion, text: "HE DOESN'T NEED FRIENDS. HE NEEDS WINS."*

**Dash vs Turbo (Heart vs Logic):**
> *Two-way interview style: Dash talking passionately about wanting to win for the fans, Turbo staring blankly, blinks slowly, says nothing. Cut to black. Text: "RACE DAY."*

### 6. FAN CAM — Community win celebrations (post when notable wins happen)

These drive belonging and FOMO. Use winner's wallet alias if they have one.

> *Stadium jumbotron showing a winner's name, crowd going wild, spotlights sweeping, confetti, [winning hamster] pointing at camera, text overlay "[USERNAME] JUST WON [AMOUNT] $HAMSTAR"*

**Caption formula:** `The wheel was kind to [username] tonight 👑 [Amount] $HAMSTAR. Who's next?`

### 7. TRAINING CAMP — Between-race lifestyle content (ongoing)

Keeps the feed alive between races. Shows hamsters as athletes with full lives.

Clip ideas:
- Pre-race weigh-in ceremony (deadly serious energy for a tiny animal)
- All three hamsters at an awkward team dinner, staring at their food
- Press conference: reporters ask questions, Flash gives nonsense answer
- Turbo doing cold plunge, expressionless, steam rising
- Dash recording a motivational video for his fans on a tiny phone
- Flash genuinely lost on the track during a practice lap
- Equipment check — Turbo inspecting wheel bearings with a magnifying glass
- Sponsor announcement — hamster holding up a tiny banner/logo

---

### 8. PROPHECY — Bold pre-race predictions (for virality)

Post before each race. Wrong predictions generate as much engagement as right ones.

> *Mystical oracle figure in dark fog, crystal ball showing three hamsters racing, dramatic music, prediction text fades in: "THE DARK HORSE STRIKES AGAIN"*

> *Newspaper front page aesthetic: "ANALYSTS PREDICT CHAOS — Flash favored for first time in 3 races"*

If wrong — post the accountability clip. Ownership is content.

```

```


## Virality Engineering

The combination that makes this formula work:

1. **Real stakes** — actual money on real animals. Not a game. That's the hook no one else has.
2. **Character arcs** — Dash/Flash/Turbo have personalities. Fans pick sides. Community forms around teams.
3. **Unpredictability** — Flash's chaos stat is 91. That's a content machine. Every race could go wrong.
4. **AI x reality** — AI-generated character content + real race footage creates a cinematic universe around real events.
5. **Dark horse narrative** — Turbo has 1 win. Every time he races, it's an underdog story. Those always go viral.

The loop: AI content builds character fandom → fans pick hamsters → they cheer with $HAMSTAR → they win or lose → they share the outcome → new people discover the game → cycle repeats.
